AWS VPN CloudHub

=> If you have multiple sites, each with its own VPN connection, you can use AWS VPN Cloudhub to connect those sites together. Its similar to VPC peering 
    in that it works on hub-and-spoke model
=> AWS VPN CloudHub is low cost and easy to manage. Though it operates over the public internet, all traffic between the customer gateway and the AWS VPN  CloudHub is encrypted.

=> Its a way of simplifying VPN Network topology.
