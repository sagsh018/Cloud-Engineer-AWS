=> RDS is for OLTP Workloads
    Great for processing a lots of small transactions, like customer orders, banking transactions, payments, and booking systems

=> Not suitable for OLAP Workloads
    Use Redshift for data wherhousing and OLAP tasks, like analysing large amounts of data, reporting, and slaes forecasting
